Who is this Alastair Leslie

Alastair Leslie is a running and endurance coach based in Wellington, New Zealand. He has a strong background in running, which led him to become a coach focused on helping athletes improve their performance. He works with runners of all levels, from beginners to more experienced athletes, helping them build fitness, improve times, and complete endurance events. His coaching is focused on steady, structured training that fits around school, work, and daily life. Alastair creates individual training plans based on each athlete’s goals, whether that is improving race results or building endurance. Through his coaching, he supports athletes in developing confidence, consistency, and long-term progress in running.
